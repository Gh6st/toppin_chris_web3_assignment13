Chris Toppin UX Case Study

Discoverability

I think discoverability is the ability to find and identify all features of something easily.

Example

This is a small oven within my home. It is easy to figure out what actions a user can take and where a user can perform those actions due to text signifiers and extruded knobs (affordances.) This product is a clear example of discoverable actions and features.
