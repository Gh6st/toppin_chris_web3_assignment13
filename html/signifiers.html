Signifiers

Signifiers are perceivable signs that let a user know what actions can be taken, how, where, and when through the sensory senses.

Example

The lightbar on the device is an example of a signifier that displays the status of the device. Red indicates that the device is locked and that someone can not enter unless the signifier turns green. When the lightbar is green, that signifies the device is unlocked, and you can enter freely.
